Mesocycle

4-week blocks. Weeks 1–3 progressively overload, week 4 deloads. Repeat with heavier loads next cycle.
Phases
Week 1Acclimation
  • RPE on compounds: 7
  • Reps: Upper end of range
  • Goal: Find working weights

Nothing should feel brutal. Set the baseline for the next 3 weeks.

Week 2Build
  • RPE on compounds: 8
  • Load: +5–10 lb on bar
  • Goal: Push the limit

Add weight or squeeze an extra rep. Real work.

Week 3Overreach
  • RPE on compounds: 8–9
  • Load: +5–10 lb again
  • Goal: Accumulate fatigue

Hardest week. Fatigue is deliberate — drives adaptation after deload.

Week 4Deload
  • RPE max: 6
  • Sets: Cut by ~40%
  • Load: Same weight

Drop volume, keep movement quality. Don't skip this — next cycle starts stronger because of it.

Progressive Overload Rules
Double Progression
If a set calls for 8–10 reps, hit 10 at RPE 8 before adding weight. Top of range clean → +5 lb next session, back to 8 reps.
Compound Loading
Bench / OHP: +2.5–5 lb / week. Squat / DL: +5–10 lb / week. Only when RPE 8 at top rep target.
Track Everything
Log weight, reps, RPE every set. This app saves to localStorage — also fine to mirror to Hevy or Strong.
Mesocycle Reset
Start next cycle at week 3 weight, RPE 7. New PRs hit weeks 2–3.
When NOT to Add
RPE 9+ before finishing reps → keep weight, chase reps instead. Form breakdown → reduce. Never sacrifice form.
Recovery
Overload only works with recovery. 7–9 hrs sleep, 0.7–1 g protein / lb bodyweight.
